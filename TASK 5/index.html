5 things to note in 'Leo' first single - 'Naa Ready'!

Vijay is gearing up for the release of his film 'Leo' which is set to release in theatres on October 19. The first single from the movie, 'Naa Ready' was launched yesterday, June 22. The movie makers launched it on Vijay's 49th birthday. Composed by Anirudh Ravichander, the song is sung by Vijay and the rap portions are sung by Asal Kolaar
